What are waves?



swap_calls

Vibrations

Waves are the vibration of particles. They vibrate in a specific way to create different types of waves, like light waves or sound waves.

swap_horiz swap_vert

2 types

There are 2 types of waves: transverse and longitudinal (compression). Transverse waves move up and down, whereas longitudinal waves move left and right.

view_list

Atributes

There are many factors that determine how the wave is percieved, such as wavelength (the space between two parts of the wave) and amplitude (the height of the wave).
